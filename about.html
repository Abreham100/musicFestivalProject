About festival

Derry music Music Festival brings world-class music to the heart of derry and northern Ireland every July, offering an inspiring programme of concerts, talks and workshops for audiences of all ages.

Across 3 days , leading international artists, rising stars, and local musicians come together in a celebration of both excellence and community. From full-length evening concerts to late-night events, short recitals, and family-friendly performances, the Festival spans classical, jazz, folka and rap offering something for everyone.
Additionally, good food and drink with good offer from our great restaurant.

with a focus on 18 - 25 years old , the festival aimed to be launchpad for emerghing artists, a showcase for a local legends and a gathering place for music lovers from derry, and Letterkenny and everyone who wants to join, we invite some international artists too.
